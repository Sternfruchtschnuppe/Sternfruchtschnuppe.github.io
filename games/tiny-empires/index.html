A classic 4X strategy game I built solo in my spare time during my studies. Explore, found cities, and grow your empire.

Technical focus

  • Full 4X gameplay loop with a fog-of-war shader and a replay system
  • Traditional and deep-learning-based AI (bachelor's thesis)
  • Multiplayer over LAN

I developed this game on my own in my spare time during my studies, to learn new aspects of gamedev, apply things I heard about in class, and have a contrast to the theory-heavy teaching at university.
