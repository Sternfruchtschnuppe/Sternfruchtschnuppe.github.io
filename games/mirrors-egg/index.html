Mirrors Egg

Unity · 3D sandbox jam

Role
Programmer · Game Design
Team
6
Duration
48 hours
Engine
Unity
Context
Game Jam
itch.io

A quirky 3D sandbox where you steer an egg through a kitchen, unlocking achievements by preparing it in silly ways.

Technical focus

  • Egg character controller and interaction mechanics (mustard, aluminum foil)
  • Object interactions with kitchen equipment: toaster, microwave, and kettle

My first game jam. Built during the Semestergamejam at TUM (Munich, Germany) in Winter 2021, with 3 programmers, 2 artists, and 1 sound designer.
